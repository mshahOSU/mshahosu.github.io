About Us

Welcome to Brows R Us, where beauty meets precision and relaxation. We specialize in creating an inviting, professional space for all your beauty needs, from eyebrow threading to rejuvenating facials. Our mission is to provide exceptional, personalized services that enhance your natural beauty and leave you feeling refreshed and confident.
Our Services

We specialize in a variety of services. Our aim is to deliver the top level of customer service based on your requests.

  • Eyebrow Threading: Our threading experts shape your eyebrows with precision to create the perfect arch, enhancing your facial features with a natural and clean look.
  • Waxing: We provide gentle yet effective waxing services for smooth, hair-free skin. Whether you're looking for a quick touch-up or a full-body treatment, we’ve got you covered.
  • Facials: Pamper your skin with our signature facials designed to refresh, hydrate, and rejuvenate. Our treatments are tailored to your skin type and designed to leave you glowing.
  • Henna Designs: We offer beautiful, custom henna designs for any occasion. Whether it's for a wedding, festival, or just because, our henna artists will create intricate and stunning artwork on your skin.

Our Story

Brows R Us was founded in 2010 by Dipali Shah, a passionate beauty professional with over 20 years of experience in the industry. As one of the first salons to perform eyebrow threading in Pittsburgh, we quickly became a go-to spot for clients seeking precision and a unique beauty experience. Over the years, we’ve expanded our services to include waxing, facials, and henna designs, but our commitment to providing exceptional care and personalized service has never wavered.
With more than two decades of experience, Dipali has mastered the art of eyebrow shaping, along with a wide range of beauty treatments, ensuring each client receives the best possible care. Her expertise and dedication to beauty have earned the salon a reputation for quality, precision, and trustworthiness.
At Brows R Us, we believe beauty is personal, and our team strives to create an environment where clients feel both relaxed and rejuvenated. Whether you’re getting a signature facial, a quick wax, or a custom henna design, our goal is to ensure you leave feeling confident, refreshed, and beautiful.
