Mehndi/Henna

We offer beautiful, custom henna designs for any occasion. Whether it's for a wedding, festival, or just because, our henna artists will create intricate and stunning artwork on your skin.

What is Mehndi/Henna?

It is a new fashion in Body art and Tattoos has been creating waves in North America and European Union, thanks to the magical herbal plant called Henna. Henna has been used to adorn the Body as part of wedding, baby shower and holiday celebration since the late Bronze Age in the eastern Mediterranean.

How Does it Work?

Mehndi/Henna is an herb that is dried and makes a powder to produce the paste that is applied to the skin in various designs. This is natural product, pain free and safe. These herbal Mehndi/tattoos have a reddish-orange color that last 1 to 2 weeks.

In Todays Culture!

Now a days, Mehndi has not only used for the traditional Weddings, Eid days and is being even applied for a Valentine's Day bash, a formal party or even an evening at a disco. Mehndi has been applied to the status of body art and as a fashion accessory for the youngsters.
