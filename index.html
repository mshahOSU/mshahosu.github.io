Our services:

Eyebrow Threading

A professional eyebrow threading has the power to completely transform your face from ordinary to amazing. Well shaped brows frame and illuminate the eyes, contour your cheekbones, and can even erase years and gives you younger look. Think surgery-free face-lift!!!

Waxing

Nearly everyone shaves or plucks unwanted hair off some part of their body. Waxing is a more professional safe way for hair removal and is used to remove a large amount of hair from the follicle in one stroke.

Facials

We offer a variety of facials including herbal facials and natural. Using a sophisticated machine, we clean, slough off the upper layers of dead skin cells, extract blackheads, detoxify, stimulate circulation, and finally revitalize the skin. Facials typically include a massage, cleansing, toning, steaming, exfoliating and moisturizing of the skin.

Mehndi/Henna

Mehndi/Henna is an herb that is dried and makes a powder to produce the paste that is applied to the skin in various designs. This is natural product, pain free and safe. These herbal Mehndi/tattoos have a reddish-orange color that last 1 to 2 weeks.
